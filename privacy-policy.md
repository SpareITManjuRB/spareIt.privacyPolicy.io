Privacy Policy for SpareIt-GMS(Garage Management System)

Last updated: 3-August-2025

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Camera Permission:
- We use camera access to allow garage owners and mechanics to take photos of vehicles, parts, damage assessment, and service documentation
- Photos are used solely for creating service records and maintaining vehicle history
- Images are stored securely on your device and/or our secure servers

Data Collection:
- Customer vehicle information (make, model, registration details)
- Service records and maintenance history
- Parts inventory data
- Appointment and scheduling information

2. HOW WE USE INFORMATION

- Document vehicle conditions and service work performed
- Create comprehensive service records and reports
- Generate professional invoices and documentation
- Maintain customer vehicle history
- Provide efficient garage management services

3. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

- All photos and data are encrypted and stored securely
- Data is backed up to protect against loss
- Access is restricted to authorized users only
- We do not share your data with third parties without consent

4. YOUR RIGHTS

- You can delete photos and data at any time
- You have full control over your garage's information
- You can request data export or deletion
- Contact us for any privacy-related concerns

5. CAMERA USAGE SPECIFICALLY

- Camera is used only when you actively choose to take photos
- Photos are used for documentation purposes only
- No automatic photo capture occurs
- All images remain under your control
